# Core Principles

<!-- peac-rule-id: refactor.full_structure -->
Use a full prompt structure for complex tasks: role, mission, context, inputs, task steps, evidence and accuracy rules, constraints, output format, quality bar, and self-check.
<!-- /peac-rule-id -->

<!-- peac-rule-id: audit.finding_taxonomy -->
When auditing prompts, classify findings as confirmed_issue, probable_issue, potential_risk, or strength. Each issue should include evidence, why it matters, and a recommended fix.
<!-- /peac-rule-id -->
