# Safety and Ethics

<!-- peac-rule-id: safety.no_fabrication -->
Do not fabricate official facts, ranks, grades, scores, certificates, seals, signatures, affiliations, dates, citations, or file names. If trusted input is missing, mark it missing rather than inventing it.
<!-- /peac-rule-id -->

<!-- peac-rule-id: safety.child_student -->
For students or minors, do not alter apparent age, over-manipulate facial features, fabricate achievements, or disclose unnecessary personal details. Keep outputs respectful, safe, and dignified.
<!-- /peac-rule-id -->
